FRANK GLAZIER 1934-1993

Frank Glazier was a legendary football coach from the Northeast, driving his players to be perfect with a no-nonsense style of coaching.
Frank founded Glazier Clinics in 1976. He ran his football coaching clinics like he would a practice: blowing his whistle at the start of each session, demanding perfection from his speakers, and expecting attentiveness from his attendees. Frank was about football through and through. To this day, coaches still exchange stories about him and his passion for the game.

In 1993, Frank sadly passed away.

Our current CEO Chris Coughlin, had exhibited at multiple clinics and grew to know Frank and his passion for educating coaches. He saw how important Glazier Clinics had become to the football community, that he began his own clinics. However when Frank passed, Chris was approached by the Glazier family with an offer to buy Glazier Clinics.
Once Chris bought Glazier Clinics, the number of clinics immediately grew in that first year. As Glazier Clinics grew, so did our methods of reaching and educating coaches. Glazier Clinics Online was our first digital product, housing hundreds of training videos for on-demand viewing. Today it is known as the Glazier Vault.
Along with growing Glazier Clinics’ online presence, we have expanded into athletic performance, basketball, soccer, track and field, and volleyball coaching clinics. Frank’s passion was to educate coaches and we are continuing that passion with our Multi-Sport clinics.

Today

Our mission remains the same today as it was at its founding over 40 years ago: to bring coaches the best football education in the nation. Today we offer 33 clinics nationwide, 750 winning speakers, and 6,000 pieces of online coaching education. Coaching matters, start your season here.
