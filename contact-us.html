FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How does the season pass work?

The Season Pass enables coaches to attend all of our clinics for one low price. The Season Pass can be purchased as an individual or as a staff. In addition to clinic attendance, Season Pass holders have year-round access to the Glazier Vault, which includes access to Glazier Clinic Online training videos, clinic notes, live webinars, eFootballFlix and Head Coach Academy.
